About Us

At Plan for Money, we bridge the gap between innovative ideas and successful businesses. Our platform connects entrepreneurs with industry-leading mentors, cutting-edge incubators, and established MSMEs to foster growth and innovation in the startup ecosystem.

Our Mission

To empower entrepreneurs by providing them with the resources, guidance, and connections they need to transform their innovative ideas into successful ventures.

Our Vision

To create a thriving ecosystem where startups, MSMEs, and incubators collaborate seamlessly to drive innovation and economic growth.

Our Values

Innovation, collaboration, integrity, and excellence guide everything we do as we help shape the future of entrepreneurship.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive support to help entrepreneurs at every stage of their journey, from ideation to scaling their business.

Mentorship Programs

Connect with experienced industry leaders who provide personalized guidance and insights to help you navigate challenges and accelerate growth.

Incubation Support

Access state-of-the-art facilities, technical resources, and expert guidance through our network of leading incubators.

Business Development

Get comprehensive support in strategy, market research, financial planning, and operational excellence.

MSME Partnerships

Collaborate with established MSMEs for market access, technology transfer, and scaling opportunities.

Educational Resources

Access workshops, webinars, and learning materials designed to enhance your entrepreneurial skills.

Networking Events

Join exclusive events and connect with fellow entrepreneurs, investors, and industry experts.
